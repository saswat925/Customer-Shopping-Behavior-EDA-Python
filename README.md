# рџ“Љ Key Business Insights

- Clothing generated the highest sales among all product categories.
- Male customers contributed more revenue than female customers.
- Fall recorded the highest seasonal sales.
- Montana generated the highest total sales.
- Medium (M) was the most preferred size.
- Green was the highest-selling product color.
- Customer spending showed a weak correlation with age, review rating, and previous purchases.
- Customers with high purchase amounts and frequent repeat purchases achieved the highest Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
